ABOUT THE CAFE

The food and beverages industry is quite robust in Egypt. Tea and coffee houses, in particular, are growing fast within Egypt. Most new entrepreneurs move toward opening cafés or restaurants as the industry is attractive for new entrants. The annual revenues of food service companies in Egyptis about $1.3 billion (EgyptGDP, 2018). Latte Café was founded by a Egyption entrepreneur, Mr. Mohamed Shalan, in June 2017. Mr. Shalan study the Egyption food and beverages market carefully and the idea of establishing Latte Café came from his experience and knowledge about the market.

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.
